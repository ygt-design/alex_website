Project 1

(New) Mechanick Exercises is a project that emerged from my interest Joseph Moxon’s 1677 book, Mechanick Exercises or The Doctrine of Handy-Works. The first ever English language book published on typography, Mechanick Exercisess is a technical manual and builder’s guide that offers detailed instructions on the construction and operation of a printing press. It also instructs the reader on the ideal physical postures and movements required to operate a press.
